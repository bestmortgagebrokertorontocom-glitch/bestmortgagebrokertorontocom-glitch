## What we do

**Best Mortgage Brokers Toronto** is an **independent guide** that helps Toronto and GTA home buyers find a mortgage broker they can actually trust — without wading through subway ads and curated five-star reviews.

We collect public Google Maps data on licensed mortgage brokerages across the Greater Toronto Area, filter by rating and review volume, then read the reviews in detail to understand what each firm is genuinely good at. The result is an editorial shortlist of the brokers we think are truly worth a call, supported by deep-dive reviews and a buyer's guide.

- ✅ **Vetted by 10,000+ real Google reviews**
- ✅ **Ranked by service quality — not advertising**
- ✅ **Written for buyers, not banks**
- 📍 Covering Toronto, North York, Scarborough, Etobicoke, Mississauga, Vaughan, Markham & beyond
